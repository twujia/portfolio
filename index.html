Background

About me.

When I finished my undergraduate degree in Psychology, I wasn't sure what was next:

Do I continue this track and become a psychologist? Is there something else I should be doing? What should I have for dinner tonight?

All of these questions lingered around my head until I came across the Master of Information Technology program at the University of Melbourne, where they offered various majors. It was then I found the Human-Computer Interaction specialisation where I saw a major overlap in my interests: observing how people behave and the rapid advancement of technology.

Thus started my career in UX/UI.

  • Big fan of earth tones (dark green in particular).
